Drinks

A drink or beverage is a liquid intended for human consumption. In addition to their basic function of satisfying thirst, drinks play important roles in human culture. Common types of drinks include plain drinking water, milk, juice, smoothies and soft drinks. Traditionally warm beverages include coffee, tea, and hot chocolate. Caffeinated drinks that contain the stimulant caffeine have a long history.

In addition, alcoholic drinks such as wine, beer, and liquor, which contain the drug ethanol, have been part of human culture for more than 8,000 years. Non-alcoholic drinks often signify drinks that would normally contain alcohol, such as beer, wine and cocktails, but are made with a sufficiently low concentration of alcohol by volume. The category includes drinks that have undergone an alcohol removal process such as non-alcoholic beers and de-alcoholized wines.

Batanga

A refreshing and classic Mexican cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and cola. Served over ice in a tall glass, it’s a smooth and simple drink that balances the citrusy tartness of lime with the sweet, fizzy complexity of cola. Often garnished with a wedge of lime, the Batanga offers a perfect combination of flavors for a crisp and easy-to-enjoy beverage.

A popular Mexican drink made with tequila, fresh lime juice, and a splash of cola. Served in a tall glass over ice, the Batanga combines the zesty tang of lime with the sweet and effervescent flavor of cola. It’s a refreshing, crisp cocktail that’s both smooth and easy to sip.

Sangria

A refreshing and fruity Spanish drink made with red wine, fresh fruit, and a blend of sweet and citrusy flavors. Typically infused with fruits like oranges, lemons, and berries, along with a splash of brandy, soda, and sweetener. Sangria is chilled and served over ice, offering a perfect balance of fruity sweetness and a hint of spice, making it a popular choice for gatherings and warm-weather sipping.

A vibrant and refreshing drink made with red or white wine, mixed with a variety of fresh fruits like oranges, lemons, and berries. Sweetened with a hint of sugar or fruit juice, and often infused with brandy for added depth of flavor. Chilled and served over ice, Sangria is a flavorful and fruity cocktail that’s perfect for summer or any social gathering.

Cape Codder

A refreshing and crisp drink made with vodka and cranberry juice. Served over ice, it’s typically garnished with a slice of lime, which adds a subtle citrus twist. The Cape Codder is known for its bright red color and tart, slightly sweet flavor, making it a popular choice for those seeking a light and invigorating cocktail.

A classic and simple cocktail made with vodka and cranberry juice, served chilled over ice. Topped with a fresh lime wedge for a hint of citrus, the Cape Codder offers a refreshing, slightly tart flavor profile. It’s a light and easy-to-enjoy drink, perfect for any occasion.

Paloma

A popular and refreshing Mexican drink made with tequila, fresh grapefruit juice, lime, and a touch of soda or sparkling water. It’s often served over ice with a hint of salt on the rim. The Paloma offers a perfect balance of tart citrus and the smoothness of tequila, making it a light and flavorful cocktail ideal for any occasion.

A refreshing and tangy Mexican cocktail made with tequila, grapefruit juice, lime, and a splash of soda water. Often garnished with a salty rim and a slice of grapefruit or lime, the Paloma balances the bright, citrusy flavors of grapefruit with the smoothness of tequila. It’s a light, crisp, and deliciously simple cocktail perfect for warm days.
